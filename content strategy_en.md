# Content Strategy for Bitcoin Value Proposition Analysis

## Core Strategy

### Content Distribution Strategy
**Detailed Core Segments (70% content):**
- **Segment 4 (Price Journey)** - Corresponds to "price changes" focus in detail.md
  - In-depth analysis of decade-long price evolution
  - Timeline annotation of major events
  - Correlation comparison with traditional financial products
  - Volatility analysis and cyclical patterns

- **Segment 5 (Winners Analysis)** - Corresponds to "big buyers" focus in detail.md  
  - Institutional holdings tracking and entry timing
  - Individual vs institutional investor behavior differences
  - Wealth distribution concentration evolution
  - "Smart money" investment strategy analysis

**Brief Supporting Segments (30% content):**
- **Segment 1 (Introduction)** - Fun facts introduction to build curiosity
- **Segment 2 (Essence)** - Technical characteristics overview to lay foundation for subsequent analysis
- **Segment 3 (Maintainers)** - Ecosystem maintainer introduction to explain system stability
- **Segment 6 (Future)** - Future trends summary to echo opening questions

## Logical Connection Framework

### Progressive Causal Chain
```
Curious Phenomenon → Understanding Essence → Ecosystem Support → Market Performance → Wealth Effect → Future Trends
       ↓                   ↓                    ↓                   ↓                 ↓              ↓
   (Brief)             (Brief)              (Brief)            (Detailed)        (Detailed)     (Brief)
```

### Transition Question Design
1. **Introduction→Essence**: "What technical principles are hidden behind this mysterious phenomenon that arouses curiosity?"
2. **Essence→Maintainers**: "Who exactly is maintaining and operating such a complex decentralized system?"  
3. **Maintainers→Price**: "How is a stably operating ecosystem reflected in market value?"
4. **Price→Winners**: "In this game of dramatic price volatility, who really seized the wealth opportunities?"
5. **Winners→Future**: "What kind of future does the current wealth pattern and market structure predict for Bitcoin?"

### Trading Volume as Connecting Thread
**Core Concept**: Use "trading volume changes" from detail.md as the data thread running through all segments

- **Segment 3 ending**: Introduce "network activity and transaction processing capacity" concept
- **Segment 4 core**: Use trading volume data to explain the internal logic of price volatility
  - "Everyone panics and flees" - Trading volume surges, selling pressure
  - "Everyone seems to think they should speculate again" - Trading volume recovers, FOMO sentiment
  - "Large companies are also positioning during this stage" - Large transactions increase, institutional entry signals
- **Segment 5 beginning**: Analyze big buyers' behavior patterns through trading volume

## Specific Implementation Plan

### Segment 4 (Price Journey) - Detailed Writing Strategy
**Data Story Focus:**
1. **Timeline Analysis**: Three major cycles of 2017, 2021, 2024
2. **Comparative Analysis**: Correlation changes with gold, S&P 500, US Dollar Index
3. **Volatility Characteristics**: Data proof of "independent asset" properties
4. **Turning Points**: Immediate and long-term impact of major events on prices

**Visualization Suggestions:**
- Interactive candlestick charts with event timelines
- Correlation heatmaps showing relationships with traditional assets
- Volatility comparison bar charts

### Segment 5 (Winners Analysis) - Detailed Writing Strategy  
**Data Story Focus:**
1. **Institutional Entry**: Timing of corporate purchases by Tesla, MicroStrategy, etc.
2. **Holdings Analysis**: Profit differences between long-term holders vs short-term traders  
3. **Concentration**: Trend changes in holdings proportion of top 1% addresses
4. **Behavior Patterns**: Correlation between large transfers and price volatility

**Visualization Suggestions:**
- Treemap showing wealth distribution
- Sankey diagram displaying capital flows
- Timeline marking major institutional entry points

### Inter-Segment Transition Design
**Brief-Detailed Transition Techniques:**
- Brief segments end with "questioning" to introduce next segment
- Detailed segments begin by echoing previous questions, establishing causal relationships
- Use connecting words like "however", "precisely because", "this also explains why"

1. **Introduction Phase**: Spark curiosity through fun facts
2. **Understanding Phase**: Quickly establish technical and ecosystem cognitive foundation  
3. **In-depth Phase**: Detailed analysis of market performance and wealth effects
4. **Summary Phase**: Future judgments based on data analysis
